Applying Assertion Checkers to Your Code

After you configure your library settings for customized checkers, customize the checkers you need for your design, and save the customized checkers to an .xml file, you can apply the checkers to your code.

Procedure

  1. Create an instance of the customized assertion checker(s) in your code.
  2. Compile your code.
