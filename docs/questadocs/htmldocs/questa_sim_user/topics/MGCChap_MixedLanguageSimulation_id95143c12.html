Mixed-Language Simulation

Questa SIM allows you to simulate designs that are written in VHDL, SystemC, Verilog, and SystemVerilog. While design units must be entirely of one language type, any design unit may instantiate other design units from another language. Any instance in the design hierarchy may be a design unit from another language without restriction.

In addition, Questa SIM supports a procedural interface between SystemC and SystemVerilog, so you may make calls between these languages at the procedural level.
