Alternate Optimization Flows

You can implement optimizations that use variations of the Three- and Two-Step flows. Although the Three-Step flow is recommended for most Questa SIM simulations, you may find these alternative variations to be useful in your environment.
Note:

QIS is the preferred method to use when optimizing designs with vopt for debug/visibility purposes. Refer to “Questa Dumping and Visibility” for complete details.
