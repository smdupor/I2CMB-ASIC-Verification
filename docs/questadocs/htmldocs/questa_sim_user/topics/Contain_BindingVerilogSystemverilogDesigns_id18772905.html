Binding to Verilog or SystemVerilog Designs

The SystemVerilog bind construct allows you to bind a Verilog or SystemVerilog design unit to a SystemC module.

This is especially useful for binding SystemVerilog assertions to your SystemC, VHDL, Verilog and mixed designs during verification. See The SystemVerilog bind Construct in Mixed-Language Designs.
