Analyzing Waveforms

The Wave window allows you to view the results of your simulation as HDL waveforms and their values.

The Wave window is divided into a number of panes (Figure 1). You can resize the pathnames pane, the values pane, and the waveform pane by clicking and dragging the bar between any two panes.
