Zooming the Waveform Display

There are numerous methods for zooming the Waveform display.

Procedure

  1. Click the Zoom Mode icon on the Wave window toolbar.
    1. In the waveform display, click and drag down and to the right.

      You should see blue vertical lines and numbers defining an area to zoom in (Figure 1).

      Figure 1. Zooming in with the Zoom Mode Mouse Pointer

  2. Select View > Zoom > Zoom Last.
    1. The waveform display restores the previous display range.
  3. Click the Zoom In icon a few times.
  4. In the waveform display, click and drag up and to the right.

    You should see a blue line and numbers defining an area to zoom out.

  5. Select View > Zoom > Zoom Full.
